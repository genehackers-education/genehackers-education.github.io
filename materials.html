Good to know

  • Several supplies are buy-once and reusable (printed card decks, glue, a UV light), so a second visit costs less.
  • The spreadsheet has live formulas — change the unit costs to match your own suppliers and the totals update.
  • Many lessons have a near-free version: beads instead of candy, recycled bottles for the water filter, printed strips for CRISPR.
