UChicago Genehackers · free outreach toolkit

Bring synthetic biology into any classroom.

Nine hands-on lessons that take kids from “what is DNA?” to designing their own engineered organism — with everything you need to actually run them. Built by a college iGEM team, free for anyone to use and change.

What's inside

Each lesson comes with a step-by-step facilitator script, a materials list with real costs, printable worksheets, debrief questions, and a slide deck. You can run one lesson or string several together into a full visit.

  • A facilitator guide — the nine lessons written out, minute by minute, including the mistakes we've already made so you don't have to.
  • Printable worksheets & handouts — codon dictionaries, monster and CRISPR cards, capstone decks, and student sheets.
  • A materials & cost sheet — what to buy and what it runs, per lesson and per visit.
  • Nine slide decks — one per lesson, ready to present.
