The lessons

All nine lessons

They're ordered to build on each other — read DNA, then edit it, then design with it — but every lesson also stands completely on its own. Mix and match to fit your class.

1

Intro: what is synthetic biology?

A 10-minute discussion opener on DNA and what synthetic biology is.

Gr. 3–6 · 10–15 min · $0
See the lesson →

Build-a-DNA double helix

Name-in-DNA bracelets

Strawberry DNA extraction

Monster genetics

CRISPR cut-and-paste

Gr. 5–6 · 30–40 min · $2–5
See the lesson →

Engineer a glowing microbe

See the lesson →
8

Clean water: sense it & solve it

See the lesson →

Design-a-synbio-solution
