Lesson 1

Intro: what is synthetic biology?

Grades
3–6
Time
10–15 min
Group
Whole class
Est. cost
$0

A short, discussion-based opener that introduces DNA and synthetic biology using real-world examples kids already recognize — then anchors the rest of the visit to a problem the students themselves want to solve.

What students do

  • Talk through what DNA is and what it does
  • See real-world synthetic biology they already know — medicine made in yeast, glowing animals, plastic-eating bacteria
  • Bring up their own questions, which the rest of the visit builds on

What you'll need

Just the slides — no supplies needed.
