Lesson 9

Design-a-synbio-solution

Grades
4-6
Time
30-40 min
Group
Small groups
Est. cost
$2-5

A capstone design challenge: students pick a real problem, combine biological "parts" (a chassis + a sensor + an output), and pitch their own engineered organism, running the same design–build–test–learn cycle as a real iGEM team. Reusable printed card decks; near-zero cost.

What students do

  • Pick a real problem, such as dirty water, plastic waste, or disease
  • Combine biological parts: a chassis + a sensor + an output
  • Sketch and pitch their own engineered organism

What you'll need

Printed parts-card and problem-card decks and a design worksheet. About $2-5 a class.

Full quantities and costs are in the materials & cost sheet.
