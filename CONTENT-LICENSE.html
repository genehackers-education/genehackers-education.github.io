License & reuse

Everything here is released under CC0 1.0 — effectively the public domain.

That means you can use it, change it, translate it, print it, and put your own organization's name and logo on it, for any purpose, with no permission needed and no attribution required.

If you found it useful, a mention of UChicago Genehackers is appreciated — but it's never required, and you should feel free to make this curriculum your own.

And if you do end up running it somewhere, we'd love to know where it went — not as a condition, just because we like seeing it travel. Tell us where you used it.
