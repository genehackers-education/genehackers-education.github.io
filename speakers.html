About

Speaker series

Alongside the classroom visits, we run a speaker series that introduces students to synthetic biology and the people who actually work in it.

The sessions are short, friendly, and aimed at making the field feel reachable — what synthetic biologists work on, how someone gets into it, and what kinds of problems the tools in this toolkit are used for in the real world.
